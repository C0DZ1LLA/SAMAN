How to Use

    Opening the System:

  1. Upon accessing the system, a welcome modal will appear.
  2. Click anywhere on the screen or the "X" button to close it and proceed.

Composing an Email:

  1. To Field: Enter recipient email address(es) in the "To:" field. Separate multiple recipients with commas.
  2. Subject Field: Input the subject of your email in the designated field.
  3. Message Field: Write your email message in the large text area provided.

Sending an Email:

  1. Click on the "Send Email" button to open your default email client. The recipient, subject, and message will be pre-filled in the email client.

Saving and Loading Templates:

  1. To save the current email content as a template: Click on the "Save Template" button.
  2. To load a saved template: Click on the "Load Template" button and enter the template index.

Creating and Clearing Templates:

  1. To populate fields with default values: Click on the "Create Template" button.
  2. To clear all input fields: Click on the "Clear Fields" button.

Adding Additional Recipients:

  1. Click on the "Add Email" button next to the recipient field.
  2. Enter additional email addresses in the popup.

Managing Saved Templates:

  1. Below the email composition section, find a list of saved templates.
  2. Click on a template button to load it into the input fields.
  3. Remove a template by clicking the "X" button next to it.

Final Note:

Enjoy using the system to streamline your email composition process!
