Pine View Hotel

Welcome to Pine View Hotel, where comfort meets tranquility amidst the breathtaking natural beauty of Cyprus. Nestled in the serene surroundings of lush green valleys and majestic mountains, our hotel offers a peaceful retreat for travelers seeking relaxation and adventure alike.

Dining

Indulge your senses with exquisite dining experiences at our hotel's restaurants and cafes. From savory dishes crafted with locally sourced ingredients to refreshing cocktails served with a smile, our culinary offerings promise to tantalize your taste buds.

House Rules

Check-in:

Check-in time is from 3:00 PM to 11:30 PM. We appreciate your cooperation in adhering to these times to facilitate a smooth check-in process.

Check-out:

Check-out time is until 11:00 AM. We kindly request that you vacate your room by this time to allow for the preparation of accommodations for our next guests.

Cancellation/Prepayment:

Cancellation and prepayment policies may vary according to the type of accommodation booked. We recommend entering your stay dates to review the specific conditions that apply to your preferred room.

Children & Beds:

Child Policies: Children over the age of 16 are welcome at Pine View Hotel.
Crib and Extra Bed Policies: Unfortunately, we do not have the capacity to provide cribs or extra beds at our property.
Age Restriction: There is no age restriction for check-in; however, please note that only children aged 16 and up are allowed to stay at Pine View Hotel.

Pets:

Pets are not allowed at our property. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation in ensuring a comfortable environment for all our guests.
